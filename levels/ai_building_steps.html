2. Steps for Building AI From Scratch

a. Step 1: Identifying the Problem & Defining Goals

Clearly understand the problem you want to solve and define measurable objectives for the AI system.

b. Step 2: Data Collection & Preparation

Gather relevant data and clean it for accuracy, consistency, and completeness. Quality data is critical for successful AI.

c. Step 3: Selection of Tools & Platforms

Choose programming languages, libraries (like TensorFlow, PyTorch), and computing resources (e.g., cloud platforms, GPUs).

d. Step 4: Algorithm Creation or Model Selection

Design your own algorithm or use existing models suited to your problem (e.g., decision trees, neural networks, clustering).

e. Step 5: Training the Algorithm or Model

Use training data to help the model learn patterns and relationships. Optimize its performance using appropriate techniques.

f. Step 6: Evaluation of the AI System

Test the model using unseen data. Evaluate accuracy, precision, recall, and other relevant metrics before deployment.
